01 / 08
Mining & Metallurgy

The mining industry is our highest-volume export sector. Caustic Soda is essential for alumina refining (Bayer process) - with every tonne of alumina requiring approximately 80–100 kg of NaOH. Sulphuric Acid is critical for copper, zinc, and nickel heap leaching. SMBS is widely used as a cyanide destruction agent in gold processing. Our primary corridors are Zambia and Congo DRC, serving copper mines and gold processing facilities.

02 / 08
Water Treatment

Rapid urbanization across Africa and the Middle East is driving massive investment in municipal water infrastructure. PAC (Polyaluminium Chloride) is the preferred coagulant in modern water treatment plants, outperforming traditional alum in turbidity removal efficiency. Caustic Soda is used for pH adjustment. SMBS neutralizes residual chlorine in final treated water. We supply WTP contractors, government utilities, and industrial effluent operators across Kenya, Nigeria, UAE, Oman, and Qatar.

03 / 08
Textile & Apparel

The global textile industry consumes massive volumes of Caustic Soda for the mercerizing process (treating cotton to increase strength and dye uptake) and Hydrogen Peroxide for bleaching. Vietnam, Indonesia, and Bangladesh represent the world's fastest-growing textile manufacturing hubs, all dependent on Indian chemical imports. Our supply relationships in these corridors are growing rapidly with demand for consistent quality and competitive FOB pricing from Gujarat ports.

04 / 08
Soap & Detergent Manufacturing

Caustic Soda Lye (liquid NaOH, 48–50% solution) is the fundamental raw material for soap saponification - the chemical reaction that converts fats and oils into soap. West Africa and East Africa have a large and growing soap manufacturing sector, with Nigeria and Kenya being major importers. Gulf countries also run significant soap and home care manufacturing operations using imported NaOH Lye. We offer competitive CIF pricing to major consuming ports.

05 / 08
Paper & Pulp

The kraft pulping process uses Caustic Soda and Sodium Sulphide to separate cellulose fibres from wood chips. Hydrogen Peroxide is the primary bleaching agent in modern ECF (Elemental Chlorine Free) and TCF (Total Chlorine Free) paper processes, replacing chlorine-based bleaching. Southeast Asia and emerging African paper sectors are strong growth markets for our chemicals.

06 / 08
Oil & Gas / Oilfield

Calcium Chloride brine is extensively used in the oil & gas industry as a completion and workover fluid due to its high density, solubility, and minimal formation damage properties. Caustic Soda is used in refineries for neutralization, gas sweetening (H₂S removal), and lube oil refining. Nigeria, UAE, Oman, and Qatar represent our primary oilfield chemical markets, supplying major drilling companies and EPCO contractors.

07 / 08
Agriculture & Fertilizer

Sulphuric Acid is a foundational input in phosphate fertilizer manufacturing (DAP - Diammonium Phosphate and MAP - Monoammonium Phosphate). Africa's growing agricultural sector requires substantial fertilizer imports, and our supply chain from Gujarat positions us to serve fertilizer blenders and distributors across East Africa. SMBS is also used as a preservative in agrochemical formulations and post-harvest food preservation.

Pharma & Food Processing

Food-grade SMBS (Sodium Metabisulfite) is widely used as an antioxidant and preservative in dried fruits, wines, seafood, and fruit juices. Food-grade Calcium Chloride is used in cheese production, vegetable processing, and beverage clarification. We hold food-grade certificates for these products and export to manufacturers requiring strict quality compliance. Pharmaceutical grade products available on special inquiry.
