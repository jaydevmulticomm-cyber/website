Buyer FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from international buyers about our documentation, quality, and export process.

Do you supply manufacturer COA or issue your own certificate?
We supply the original manufacturer's Certificate of Analysis from Grasim, GACL, DCM Shriram, Kutch Chemical, or Reliance - whichever plant the material is sourced from. The COA references the manufacturer's batch number and is issued by their accredited internal laboratory. We do not issue our own COA to avoid any authenticity questions with LC banks or port authorities.
How quickly can you ship after Purchase Order confirmation?
For standard Caustic Soda (flakes/lye) and Sulphuric Acid orders, we typically load and ship within 7–14 working days of confirmed PO and LC receipt (or TT advance). For specialty products or large volumes, lead time is 14–21 working days. We will confirm exact vessel ETA and shipping schedule in the Proforma Invoice.
Can you arrange pre-shipment inspection (PSI)?
Yes, absolutely. We facilitate pre-shipment inspection by SGS India, Bureau Veritas, or Intertek at the loading port (Hazira or Mundra). PSI covers quantity verification, sampling, visual inspection, and loading supervision. PSI costs are typically borne by the buyer. We recommend PSI for orders above 100 MT and for new trading relationships. PSI report is included in the final document pack.
What HS Codes do your products ship under?
Our primary products ship under: Sulphuric Acid - HS 2807.00; Caustic Soda (Flakes) - HS 2815.11; Caustic Soda (Lye/Liquid) - HS 2815.12; SMBS - HS 2832.20; Hydrogen Peroxide - HS 2847.00; PAC - HS 2827.32; Calcium Chloride - HS 2827.20. We confirm the exact HS Code on each Commercial Invoice and can provide HS-Code specific documentation if required by destination customs.
Do you handle LC (Letter of Credit) transactions?
Yes. LC is our preferred payment method for new buyers and large orders. We work with irrevocable LCs confirmed through Indian scheduled banks. We review all LC terms before acceptance to ensure we can comply fully - eliminating discrepancy risk. We accept 100% at sight LC and usance LC (30, 60, or 90 days). Our bank details and LC instructions are provided with the Proforma Invoice.
Is the product IMDG/UN certified for hazardous cargo?
Yes. Sulphuric Acid (UN1830 / Class 8 Corrosive), Hydrogen Peroxide (UN2014 / Class 5.1 Oxidiser), and Liquid Chlorine (UN1017 / Class 2.3) are all shipped in UN-certified containers meeting full IMDG requirements. Container placards, IMDG declarations, and DG (Dangerous Goods) manifests are provided with every hazmat shipment. Our forwarder handles all DG booking formalities with the shipping line.
Can you provide samples before we place a full order?
Yes, samples are available for most products - typically 500g to 2kg sealed in lab-grade containers with the manufacturer's original labels. Samples are shipped by courier (FedEx / DHL) with COA and MSDS. Courier cost is borne by the buyer. For Caustic Soda, Sulphuric Acid, and SMBS, samples are available within 5–7 working days of sample order confirmation. Note: hazmat products have shipping restrictions for courier and must be sent as non-hazardous quantity exceptions.
