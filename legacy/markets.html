East & Central Africa

Our highest-priority export region. Tanzania serves as a regional hub with Dar es Salaam as the main entry point for landlocked Zambia and Congo. We have established relationships with mining operators and chemical distributors across this corridor.

West Africa

Nigeria is West Africa's largest economy and a major importer of industrial chemicals - particularly Caustic Soda for soap and detergent manufacturing (Nigeria has one of Africa's largest soap industries), Sulphuric Acid for battery manufacturing, and PAC for the growing water treatment sector. Lagos Apapa is the primary receiving port. Ghana, Ivory Coast, and Senegal are secondary markets with growing chemical import demand.

Region 03 · Active Hub
● Active Corridor
UAE & MENA Region

Jebel Ali in Dubai is the world's largest man-made harbour and serves as the primary re-export hub for the entire GCC (Gulf Cooperation Council) region and broader Middle East. We supply Caustic Soda and Sulphuric Acid to UAE-based chemical distributors who serve downstream industries across Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, and Bahrain. Calcium Chloride has strong demand in GCC oilfield operations. Transit time from Hazira to Jebel Ali is just 5–8 days - our fastest corridor.
