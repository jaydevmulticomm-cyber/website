# Manufacturer Validation & Authenticity Skill

## Purpose
Ensure that every product claim, image, and testimonial on the Jaydev website visibly demonstrates partnership with top-tier Indian chemical manufacturers. Never position as a generic trader or reseller.

## Core Principle

**EVERY PAGE MUST ANSWER: "Where does this come from?"**

**✓ Correct Framing:**
- "Sourced directly from GACL – India's leading NaOH producer"
- "Reliance Caustic Soda – certified for export to 30+ countries"
- "PAC from Kutch manufacturers – trusted by WTP projects in Africa & GCC"

**✗ Avoid:**
- "Industrial chemicals" (too generic)
- "We supply caustic soda" (no manufacturer attribution)
- "Available from suppliers" (implies middleman role)

### How to Verify
- Visit manufacturer's official website → check export certifications.
- Request COA (Certificate of Analysis) from Jaydev for each batch.
- Cross-check HS codes and trade database (Google "HS code caustic soda").
- For large orders: Request pre-shipment inspection from 3rd-party lab.

## Imagery & Visual Rules

### Product Photos
- **✓ Recommended**: 
  - Actual product packaging (Jaydev-sourced shipment).
  - Manufacturer facility exterior (generic industrial background, no faces).
  - Product spec sheets or catalogs (manufacturer-branded).
  
- **✗ Avoid**:
  - Generic stock photos of "chemicals" or "industry".
  - Photo of random beakers/flasks.
  - Faces of workers (privacy + no added value).
  - Competitor or unlicensed imagery.

### Logo Usage
- **Obtain Permission**: Before using GACL, Grasim, Reliance logos, reach out to their communications/brand team.
- **Attribution**: Include small text: "Sourcing partner" or "Product sourced from [Manufacturer]".
- **Size**: Don't make manufacturer logos larger than Jaydev logo (respect brand hierarchy).

### Testimonials & Case Studies
- **Real testimonials**: Only use quotes from actual customers (anonymized if needed for confidentiality).
- **Example**:
  ```
  "Jaydev's supply of PAC from Kutch manufacturers has been consistent and on-time for our 
  WTP projects in Kenya. Professional team, responsive via WhatsApp." 
  - Procurement Manager, East African Water Utility [Name withheld]
  ```
- **Avoid fabricated testimonials** (builds distrust long-term).

## Red Lines (Never Do)

🚫 **DO NOT:**
- Claim "We manufacture chemicals" (we source, we don't make).
- Use fake manufacturer logos or claim false partnerships.
- Display generic "chemical factory" images without attribution.
- Overstate certifications (e.g., "FDA approved" if only GACL COA exists).
- Hide manufacturer info in fine print (transparency builds trust).
- Use testimonials from non-existent customers.

## Quality Control Checklist

Before publishing any page:
- [ ] Does it clearly state the manufacturer source?
- [ ] Are all product images real (not stock photos)?
- [ ] Are all testimonials from real customers (documented)?
- [ ] Are all certifications and HS codes accurate?
- [ ] Do all manufacturer names match official spellings (GACL, not Gacl)?
- [ ] Is the tone "trusted partner" (not "middleman" or "trader")?
- [ ] Are logos used with permission and proper attribution?
