# SEO & AEO Content Guidelines

This document describes how to write and structure content for search, AI, and buyer intent.
The focus is on industrial chemical exports, global markets, and trust-based conversion.

## Priorities

- Optimize for export buyers in GCC, Africa, Southeast Asia, and global industrial markets.
- Support keywords for Indian chemical exports, specific products, and compliance documentation.
- Build content for both search engines and AI assistants.

## Content Structure

- Use a single H1 per page.
- Follow with H2/H3 headings that reflect buyer questions and product needs.
- Keep paragraph length short and readable.
- Use bullet points for product specs, market advantages, and logistics.
- Include meaningful internal links across pages.

## Metadata

- `title`: concise, keyword-focused, and branded.
- `description`: clearly explain what the page delivers and why the buyer should act.
- Open Graph: image, title, description, type `website`.
- Twitter: same title/description, card type `summary_large_image`.
- Canonical: ensure one canonical URL per route.

## Schema Markup

Add JSON-LD for:
- Organization
- BreadcrumbList
- FAQPage (quality or quote pages)
- Product or Service if applicable

## Keywords and Buyer Intent

Use these concept groups:
- Chemical export trade from India
- Industrial chemicals: Sulphuric Acid, Caustic Soda, SMBS, Hydrogen Peroxide, PAC, Calcium Chloride
- Manufacturers: Grasim, GACL, DCM Shriram, Reliance, Kutch Chemical
- Export terms: FOB Hazira, CIF Mundra, UN-certified packaging, COA, MSDS
- Markets: GCC, Middle East, East Africa, West Africa, Southeast Asia

## AI SEO / AEO Best Practices

- Write with clear entity signals and structured context.
- Use explicit buyer phrases like “request quote,” “export-grade chemical supplier,” and “international shipment.”
- Answer common questions directly in page sections.
- Add FAQ entries that map to real buyer concerns.
- Use clean markup and avoid hidden or duplicated text.

## Content Tone

- Professional and precise.
- Confident but not inflated.
- Focus on reliability, sourcing, compliance, and speed.
- Avoid vague claims; prefer verifiable export details.

## Conversion Messaging

- Reinforce trust with certifications, documentation, and source manufacturers.
- Use strong CTAs like “Request quote,” “Get export pricing,” and “Contact via WhatsApp.”
- Highlight responsive service and 24–48 hour turnaround.

## Maintenance

- Keep page copy updated to match product availability and export regions.
- Preserve all company contact data in a central source file.
- Use consistent naming for products and markets across all pages.
